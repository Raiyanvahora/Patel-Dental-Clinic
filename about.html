About Our Clinic

Your Trusted Dental Care Partner in Surat

Established in Mota Varachha, Surat, Patel Dental Clinic & Implant Centre has been providing exceptional dental care to families in Varachha and surrounding areas. Our clinic is located at Shop No. 115, Green View Heights, making it easily accessible for patients across the region.

We believe everyone deserves a healthy, beautiful smile. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of dental treatments — from routine checkups and cleanings to advanced dental implants and cosmetic procedures — all at affordable prices.

Sterilized & Safe Environment

We follow strict sterilization protocols with UV sterilization and autoclave equipment to ensure a completely safe treatment environment.

Affordable Care

Quality dental care shouldn't break the bank. We offer competitive pricing and free checkups every Friday.
